Common Questions

Good to know.

What areas do you service? +
I live in the Cobban neighbourhood of Milton and primarily serve fellow Cobban neighbours. If you're nearby or in a surrounding area, send me a message and I'll let you know if I can help!
Do I need to provide tools or supplies? +
Nope! I bring my own mower, trimmer, and everything needed. You just need to be home or leave the gate open if you have one.
How do I pay? +
Cash or e-transfer, whichever works best for you. Payment is due once the work is complete.
What if it rains on my booking day? +
No worries — I'll text you and we'll reschedule for the next available slot. Wet grass doesn't cut well, so it's better for everyone.
Can I book regular weekly or biweekly service? +
Absolutely. Many of my clients are on a recurring schedule. Just mention it in your booking notes and we can set it up.
